# Walmart Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Walmart weekly sales data across 45 stores using Python, SQL Server, Excel, and Power BI. The objective was to identify revenue drivers, evaluate store performance, measure holiday sales impact, and develop actionable business insights through data analytics and visualization.

## Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
* SQL Server
* Microsoft Excel
* Power BI
* GitHub

## Dataset

The dataset contains 6,435 weekly sales records from 45 Walmart stores and includes:

* Store
* Date
* Weekly Sales
* Holiday Flag
* Temperature
* Fuel Price
* CPI
* Unemployment

## Business Questions

* Which stores generate the highest revenue?
* How concentrated is revenue among top-performing stores?
* What impact do holidays have on sales performance?
* Do economic indicators influence weekly sales?
* Which stores demonstrate the most consistent performance?

## Python Analysis

Performed exploratory data analysis including:

* Data validation and quality checks
* Summary statistics
* Revenue ranking by store
* Average sales comparisons
* Correlation analysis
* Holiday sales impact analysis
* Sales volatility analysis

### Key Findings

* Total Revenue: $6.74 Billion
* Average Weekly Sales: $1.05 Million
* Top 10 Stores generated 39.05% of total company revenue
* Holiday periods increased sales by approximately 7.84%
* Economic indicators showed minimal correlation with sales performance
* Store-specific factors appeared to be the strongest drivers of revenue variation

## SQL Analysis

Developed SQL queries to:

* Calculate total revenue and average sales
* Rank stores by performance
* Measure revenue concentration
* Evaluate holiday sales impact
* Analyze sales volatility and consistency
* Generate executive-level KPIs

## Power BI Dashboard

Dashboard features include:

* Total Revenue KPI
* Average Weekly Sales KPI
* Store Count KPI
* Holiday Sales Lift KPI
* Revenue by Store Visualization
* Holiday vs Non-Holiday Sales Analysis
* Top Store Rankings
* Executive Summary Insights

## Business Impact

Analysis revealed a moderate concentration of revenue among top-performing locations, with the top 10 stores generating nearly 40% of total sales. Holiday periods consistently increased sales performance, while macroeconomic indicators demonstrated limited influence on weekly revenue. Findings suggest that operational execution and local market factors play a greater role in store performance than broader economic conditions.
